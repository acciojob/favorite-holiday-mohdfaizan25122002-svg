Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is famous for its snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and pleasant weather, making it a perfect holiday destination.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports like paragliding and skiing.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for its breathtaking snow views.
  3. Hadimba Temple – A historic temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for its cafes and peaceful vibe.
  5. Vashisht Hot Springs – Natural hot water springs with healing properties.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali dish with rich flavors.
  • Thukpa – A hot noodle soup perfect for cold weather.
  • Trout Fish – Fresh river fish cooked with local spices.
  • Momomos – Steamed dumplings served with spicy chutney.
  • Mittha – A sweet dish made with rice and dry fruits.
